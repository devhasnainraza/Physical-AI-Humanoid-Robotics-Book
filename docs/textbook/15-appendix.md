# Appendix: Notation

## Vectors & Matrices
*   $x, y, z$: Scalars (lowercase).
*   $\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{y}$: Vectors (bold lowercase).
*   $A, R, T$: Matrices (uppercase).

## Coordinate Frames
*   \{A\}: Frame A.
*   $^A P$: Point P expressed in Frame A.
*   $^A_B T$: Transform from Frame B to Frame A.

## Dynamics
*   $q$: Generalized coordinates (Joint angles).
*   $\dot{q}$: Joint velocities.
*   $\tau$: Joint torques.
*   $M(q)$: Mass Matrix.
*   $C(q, \dot{q})$: Coriolis Matrix.
*   $G(q)$: Gravity Vector.
